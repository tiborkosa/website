My name is Tibor. For most of my life, I lived in a small village in Transylvania. After studying 2 years of Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Babes-Bolyai, I decided to move to United States. Since then, I have lived in New Mexico, Wyoming, Arizona, and New Jersey and worked as a professional ski instructor, project manager in construction, and a sommelier. One day, I realized that I was craving an intellectual challenge and went back to school to finish what I originally started. It was not easy to simultaneously work and study full time, but it was worth it. I graduated May 2015 with a GPA of 3.82, earning a diploma in A.A.S. focusing on Web Development and Management.
