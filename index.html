Hello,
My name is Tibor. I'm a web/mobile app developer. I’m also a chef, martial artist, advanced skier, sommelier, motivator, handy man who enjoys nature, animals and intellectual challenges.

For most of my life, I lived in a small village in Transylvania. After studying 2 years of Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Babes-Bolyai, I decided to move to United States. Since then, I have lived in New Mexico, Wyoming, Arizona, and New Jersey.

One day, I realized that I was craving an intellectual challenge and went back to school to finish what I originally started. It was not easy to simultaneously work and study full time, but it was worth it. I graduated May 2015 with a GPA of 3.82, earning a diploma in A.A.S. focusing on Web Development and Management.

After graduation, I learned javaScript beyoned the basics and its high demanded libraries, like jQuery, Node.js and Angular.js. Polished PhP skills and database knowledge. Now I decided to learn Phyton, Ruby on Rails and C# (for Unity 3D or .net framework). My favorite Object Oriented language is Java and when I'm not working on websites I like to perfect my Java skills as well.

This website was created from scratch. No downloaded plug-ins, no Wordpress themes and no Bootstrap assistance. That doesn’t mean I don’t know how to work with them.
